ITSA Matrix Hackathon

Rules

The SPIT Hackathon entails the following:

  • Venue:Sardar Patel Institute Of Technology :4th Floor
  • Duration :24Hrs!
  • Each team should have a minimum of 1 participant and a maximum of 4 participants.
  • Participants must select one from the following fields of interests from which a problem statement will be given to them:
    • OCR
    • IoT
    • Block Chain
  • High-speed Wi-Fi will be provided.
  • All the participants will receive a participating certificate.
  • The winners will also get the winning certificate.
  • Mentors from IBM will be present to help the participants with their solutions.
  • The students are allowed the freedom to develop mobileapplications like Android or IoS, desktop applications or websites using whichever language they feel comfortable with.

Note: Participants from colleges other than SPIT are allowed to stay and code in the campus only until 8 PM after which they will have to vacate the allotted room.
